About Me.

Hi! I'm Gabby. Born and raised in Mississippi, I've called Nashville home for the past 5 years. I live here with my husband, son, cat and dog. My full-time job is stay at home mom, but I'm also a wedding and lifestyle photographer. A few months into stay at home mamahood, I decided I needed to expand my skills if I was going to continue to stay home with my son. Being a mama is a job in and of itself, but I also wanted to help provide for my family. I stumbled upon HTML and CSS and I was hooked. I love being able to create, but I'm also super analytical and love solving puzzles. This is what makes website development so fun!
Fun fact: I'm an Enneagram type 5w6 which means I'm a natural problem solver. Let me help solve yours.
